Gods Plan For Man

Rev. Rose M. Aluli

"God has a plan to bring man back on the same level as Christ." Rose M. Aluli

This message was given To the Church on September 23, 1979.

Rose sings "Rock of Ages."

Amen. Amen. Thank you, Lord, for the blood that saves us. We worship you, Lord. We adore you, Lord. We love you, Lord. Hallelujah, Lord, that you called us to know thee. For the great plan of salvation, Lord, thank you. Thank you, Lord, for the redemption of our body, soul, and spirit, Lord, for the great plan of salvation, the redemption of the world, Lord. Hallelujah, that thou hast died that all men and women everywhere might be forgiven for all sin, Lord, that they might know thee in the power of thy resurrection. Hallelujah, Lord. Praise your name, Lord.

I thank you for this, Jesus, that you said the blood would never lose its power, Lord, but it will stand through the ages and ages and ages, Lord. Thy blood is sufficient for salvation of all men everywhere. I thank you for this, Lord. I thank you that you came in a flesh body, Lord, that you were willing to live on this earth among sin, Lord, and could be a pure sacrifice for our sin through thy blood. Thank you, Lord, for such a plan.

Blessed Lord, praise your name, Lord. Praise your name, Jesus. Hallelujah. Hallelujah. Hallelujah.

Through it all, the Lord has a plan that he is fulfilling. That is what we are going to talk about – man and what God's plan is for man, and how far we have missed the mark, the world in general.

pp. 1

Now God is doing a work among his people, and he is not disappointed in what he is doing. He is going to fulfill everything that his Word says he will fulfill. And everyone that will accept it, God knows it in his heart and he will give them the opportunity. Now that is for sure. He will not let one that has a desire for him ever be rejected. He will never reject one. And only he knows the heart. With all of that, he stands by. Just like he is here today. Amen. And I am happy. I am really happy today in the Lord because I know that he is here, and I know that he has anointed the Word, and I know that there are things in here that he wants us to know. It will be some things different from what perhaps some of us understand. Just hang on. If you are sincere with God and honest with God, it will be revealed to you in his time, Amen.

Adam was created in the image and likeness of God. Now that is saying a whole lot. They were pure, absolutely pure and holy and set over the works of God's hands. I want to show you how limited Adam was, but there is another group that is coming along. Amen.

Genesis 1:26-31 And God said, After God had made all the cattle, and made the earth, first. Let us make man in our image, after our likeness: and let them First he said 'let us make man' and then he said 'and let them' because he knew that there was going to be a creation out here. have dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over the cattle, and over all the earth, Now he limited it to earth, did you see this? He did not let Adam have rule over all of creation, over all the heavenlies and things. But there is a man, Christ Jesus, that we will rule and reign with, according to scripture; that will have dominion over all of the works of his hands, for Adam was limited. over all the earth, That’s as far as he could go. and over every creeping thing that creepeth upon the earth. So God created man in his own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them. And God blessed them, and God said unto them, Be fruitful, and multiply, and replenish the earth, and subdue it: and have dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over every living thing that moveth upon the earth. And God said, Behold, I have given you every herb bearing seed, which is upon the face of all the earth, and every tree, in the which is the fruit of a tree yielding seed; to you it shall be for meat. And to every beast of the earth, and to every fowl of the air, and to every thing that creepeth upon the earth, wherein there is life, I have given every green herb for meat: and it was so.

So they had everything that was upon the earth. But that was as far as their dominion went. Then with Christ, he said that he would give us dominion over all of the works of his hands, not just the earth. So this is no wonder that he said 'greater works than these shall ye do,' because Christ worked on the earthly plane also, as Adam had, as a flesh man. But you notice, the minute he stepped out of that flesh what happened to him. He walked through doors. He had dominion over every thing. He appeared just anywhere out of nowhere. That was a different thing again. Here is that complete likeness of God. So there is a difference. Now I can understand why he said 'greater works than these shall ye do for I go to my Father'.

We have not seen this yet, but it is coming, Amen, because the Word promised it. We cannot just take a portion of this. We have to take every bit of it, every bit of the Word. We have to take the whole thing. Oh, we do not have to. I mean we can go on blindly, but there is no limit to God. There is no end to God. He is forever and forever and forever. And there are things that we are just scraping the surface of, that is all – of the knowledge of God.

pp. 2

That little article I read about the galaxies and the black holes is just blowing scientist's minds at the expanse out here that they cannot even begin to figure out. I can remember years ago when they thought they had it pretty well harnessed, that they knew just about how the earth was created and how man was created. Now it is blowing their minds because it is too big. The bigger the telescope, the farther they see. The more satellites they put up, the more is revealed. It is clear out of man's hands now. I heard a Christian scientist on TV the other day, and he said that it is clear beyond anything that man can ever think. He said the true scientists know that there had to be a Creator; that this did not just happen, because it is too exact. Everything is moving exactly in its proper orbit. And everything is right on the second like the sun. You set your clock by the sundial, Amen. You see, man cannot do that because we are just flesh, but it takes a God Almighty to do these things.

So he put Adam over the works of his hands on the earth here, until disobedience came, then death and decay set in. The minute they disobeyed God, the minute they broke the commandment that the Lord told them not to eat of that fruit in the garden, the minute they went ahead and obeyed Satan when Satan tempted them, then the sentence of death was set upon them and the decay of the flesh set in that beautiful creation. And they were driven from the garden to go out and work as we do now, by the sweat of their foreheads, by the sweat of their face.

If we can see now what is happening here, God is about to bring a people back to have the dominion over all of the works of his hands, not just the earth. This is just one little speck as an example of God's creation. And this is where it is at, right here on the earth for an example.

This is where Jesus Christ came and died and shed his blood for our salvation: not just to save, although that is his greatest concern – the salvation of souls, but that he might produce a people that would be so dedicated to God and so completely in the will of God, that God could transform them. That they would become like unto the Son of God himself, Jesus Christ: that by his Spirit he could dwell in them and completely take over this many-membered body out in the earth here, him being the head, because Christ was in God and God was in Christ. You cannot separate the two. We know there are two, but they are one, in a sense. Because they think alike. They act alike. He came in the earth to represent the Father, didn’t he? That is what he said. He says he does nothing but to please the Father.

pp. 3

And instead of pure water or spirit, then it became bitter and sweet. Like it says in James, bitter and sweet water should not come out of the same vessel. It should be all pure. Now Adam became a flesh man, where before he was a spirit man. He was made in the image and likeness of God. So we know that he was a spirit man. And when God breathed the life into him, that was the spirit of life. It was God's own spirit in Adam. Then he gave him that commandment not to touch this thing, and he did. So then God said, now you will have to die for this. And so everyone that is born since that time, is born under the curse of Adam.

We have to be redeemed by Christ's blood. We cannot even come in contact with God if we are not redeemed by the blood of Christ, which takes away all sin, forgives all sin. But then there is a work to do in us by the Spirit, that has to overcome all the nature of Adam in us. That has to go. All of this Adam nature must go, in order for us to come into that relationship where Adam was, even to control just the earth. Do you get what I am saying?

Then we see in the earth, (I don’t know how the earth was. It doesn’t say too much about it before Adam sinned.) But then it said that thorns and briars and thistles started growing in the earth. That was part of the curse. That is why he said he would have to till the ground by the sweat of his face. And it choked out all the good, or spirit, and death came to mankind. All of these things, while they were growing – briars and thistles and things – out here in the field, there was also bitterness and hatred and all of these bad things coming in his flesh nature. He was pure, so it was a reverse action. We are born like that, but that reverse action had to come in Adam, until he became worse instead of better, because of the curse that came upon him.

This man is again being restored into the likeness of the Father and Son through Christ's death and resurrection. Now if he had not been resurrected, it could not have been completed. This process could not have been completed. But he started out as a flesh man, a baby, and grew into manhood, and then gave his life for the sins of the world. Then if he had just died, we could have been forgiven and that would have been the end of it. There would have been no resurrection because he was the firstfruits of them that slept. So when he was resurrected from the dead, he became the victor over death so that we would not have to die. There will be a people that will be caught up, that will be with the Lord in the spirit and they will not die. And we know that that people is alive in the earth today. We know that because we know that the timing in God is now, and according to the signs, we know that this is true; that there will be many alive to the coming of the Lord.

The process of Adam is being reversed. We were born in sin. So instead of, like Adam, coming from purity and going into sin, we are born in sin and become pure by the obedience to the Word right here, with the Spirit, after we have accepted Christ, if we walk in the knowledge of the scriptures. This is the only book we have that says anything about God. Many men have written many things about God, but that is not the Bible, Amen. Only by the Bible, the only book that was protected and cared for by God and watched over. I believe every word in this book, every one of them, Amen, because I have tried it and tried it and tried it. I have proven that the Word is truth. Amen.

pp. 4

We are born in sin in Adam. And as we accept Christ, or the life giving Spirit, which Adam received after he was created and God breathed life into him, Christ starts the reverse action. Through disobedience, Adam started death working in him. And I want to tell you something else. After we are saved and baptized in the Holy Ghost, and we continue in sin, that reverse action starts again in us. If we do not repent of that sin and disobedience, then that death reverse action starts out again in us. We know some who have that working in them; who have started and then back off and run back into the world. We see that reverse action start to come in their lives, in their bodies, in their health. All sorts of things start happening to them. That is that curse that was placed upon man. It starts out again. Sin causes it.

Through Christ, we receive not only eternal life, but life. Now I have scriptures for all of this when we get into the scriptures later on. Through Christ, we receive life and the process starts to change us back to that immortal being Adam started with. Now here is the point that I want to get out. He says when this mortal puts on immortality, this mortal flesh, that is a flesh body, this mortal, we are mortal or flesh. In the scriptures it says, "This mortal will put on immortality" that is, without death. There will just be a change. There will just be a change in the flesh. It is very clear. It is all through the scriptures. You do not have to search hard to find it. It is everywhere in the scriptures. And like I said, there is a people living today that will come into this immortality in God's timing, Amen. Through obedience to the Word that God left for us or pattern of examples from past history of men's lives. Now we know the scriptures were given for instruction and so on and so forth and that all of these old happenings in the Old Testament by holy men of God and the wicked also, were given for examples unto us. The Word says so. If we obey the truth in the Word, and what is the Word, didn’t he say the Word is Christ?

John 1:1-5 In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God. The same was in the beginning with God. All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made. In him was life; and the life was the light of men. And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not. Who did Jesus say he was? The light of the world. So we know what the Word is. It is Christ. And also, he is eternal life, our very life, and he wants us to know we have to come into this place to where we know what he spoke to me, "Because I live, you shall live also." He was not talking about just this natural physical life or eternal life, but we have to become obedient to the place to where the very life that he lives inside of us is our very physical life as well. We are being changed daily in his life that is in here, just like the Spirit was in Adam. That was his life until he sinned. Now remember this is the reverse action. We are gradually coming back into that place to where that life that Adam had by the Spirit, by the breath that was breathed into him. When you look at some people and it looks like they are getting younger, it is because it is the life of Christ that is reversing the action of death in them. It is the reverse action by a plan that God has. Don’t ask me how he does it. Who knows.

I just know that the Word says that it happens, and that it is happening. And that is his plan and we cannot change it. We do not have to accept it, if we do not want to. We can be critics. But if we just accept the simple Word of God like he wanted us to, then we can come into these things.If we accept the Word and obey the truth of the Word, which is life, we will find our way back into that perfect relationship Adam had in the beginning, where sin and death are destroyed. The last enemy to be put down is death. That is scripture. That is the Word. Now if you cannot receive the Word for what it says, then there is no need of you listening, Amen. But this is the Word of God.

Now Jesus said that he died that all sin, sickness, death, and so forth could be overcome in him. He did not die for each one of those things separately. It is all in the same package. If you are saved, you have just as much right to claim a healing, victory over sin, and victory over death, and resurrection if you have died, to be resurrected in the end. That is all just one death. He did not die a half a dozen times. That was all in one package. We have just gone on and not believed the Word of God. We have a perfect right to claim healthy strong bodies, a sound mind, Amen, the mind of Christ in us. Sickness, sin, death, and resurrection: he died for those very purposes. Now if he died for that, I do not care if you know a millionaire friend and you are hungry and he says just help yourself and you do not do it, it is just the same thing here. The Lord made all these provisions for us. But if we do not obey the Word, if we do not hear the Word, then we are the losers. And that is the reason the children of Israel could not go on into Canaan. We know that. Because of disobedience.

And unbelief is the greatest sin. When God tells us something, God Almighty who would never back up on his Word. I’m trying to keep quiet, but I’m stirring inside. Because he has done so much for us and we do not accept it. The most horrible thing is for God to make bold statements – I mean he has really made some rash promises in here – and for us not to believe it and accept it. It is like throwing cold water into his face, because we cannot accept his Word. How would you feel if you just continually promised your children something and they just went on as though you had not even said a word? They would say, "Daddy, can I have this? Daddy can I have that?" You would say, "Yes, yes, I promised it to you. Yes." But they still never hear you and they say, "Daddy can I have this? Daddy, can I have that?" Wouldn't you soon get disgusted with them? You would feel like taking what they did have away from them. Everything to make our way back to that relationship and beyond the relationship of Adam was in the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

1 Corinthians 15:12-22 Now if Christ be preached that he rose from the dead, how say some among you that there is no resurrection of the dead? But if there be no resurrection of the dead, then is Christ not risen: And if Christ be not risen, then is our preaching vain, and your faith is also vain. Yea, and we are found false witnesses of God; because we have testified of God that he raised up Christ: whom he raised not up, if so be that the dead rise not. For if the dead rise not, then is not Christ raised: And if Christ be not raised, your faith is vain; ye are yet in your sins. Do you see that? If this is not real truth, that there would be a resurrection, and Christ did not rise; if they say like the Jews say, that he was stolen; they stole his body – and that is what they believe to this day, that he was a false Messiah – then our faith is in vain and we are still in our sins. If he did not really rise from the dead on the third day like he said he did, then we are still in our sins. We have no Savior. But we know that we have a Savior because our lives are changing and the desires that we used to have, we do not have anymore. Amen. We desire to be with God's people. That is the one way that you can tell, when you have that desire to be with God's people and you love God's people. Then they also which are fallen asleep in Christ are perished. If he did not rise from the dead. If in this life only we have hope in Christ, we are of all men most miserable.

Even though he died for our sins, if he had not been resurrected, we would just be miserable because we do not receive enough out of this life. We are looking for the life when we will be changed and become immortal. But now is Christ risen from the dead, and become the firstfruits of them that slept. He was the first one to come back in a new body, in the heavenly body, the glorified body. Do not forget that he ate with them and drank with them. So we do not understand all of this, how a body that can take food and digest it, can walk through the walls and appear and disappear, but nevertheless it is so, because the Bible says so. He said when we look at the things that are seen, the more real things are the things that you cannot see. That is what he said, but we are so used to just these eyes that see everything, so we limit God to what we can see. How stupid we are.
